To Circuit or Not To Circuit?

Circuits in remote matches require very specific patterns, and sometimes it is more worth it to not score them at all! This is a tool for determining whether you should score a circuit or not in a remote match.
You can either input the amount of cones you can score to compare circuit and non-circuit scoring, or find the threshold for when going for a circuit is not worth it.
Note: this tool assumes that all cones are scored in teleop. Auto support will come soon.
